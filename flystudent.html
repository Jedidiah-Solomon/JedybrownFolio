FlyStudent

Revolutionizing how students connect with universities worldwide

The Problem

International students struggle with fragmented communication, missed deadlines, and disorganized application processes when reaching out to university supervisors.

  • No centralized platform for tracking communications
  • Difficulty managing multiple applications
  • Missed opportunities due to poor organization

Our Solution

FlyStudent provides a unified workspace for student-supervisor communication and application management.

  • Smart email tracking with response analytics
  • Application progress visualization
  • Integrated meeting scheduling
  • AI-powered guidance

Mission

To democratize access to higher education by streamlining communication between students and university supervisors globally.

Vision

Become the essential platform that bridges the gap between ambitious students and educational opportunities worldwide.
